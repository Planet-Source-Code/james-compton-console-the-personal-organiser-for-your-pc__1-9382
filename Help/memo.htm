Memo

This section allows memos to be created of important events or just simple reminders. The text is saved whenever you alter it, so no 'LOAD' or 'SAVE' is required. The only commands are 'Create' a memo or 'Delete' a memo. Upon clicking on 'Create', you will be asked for a subject title. This will be what is displayed in the box above the main memo....also used as the filename. The memo will then be created and stamped with today's date and the time you created the memo. You can then begin typing.
